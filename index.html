Rentivo Privacy Policy

Property Management & Rental Accounting Platform

Overview

Rentivo is a modern property management platform designed to help owners, landlords, and managers organize properties, tenants, rent tracking, and accounting records in a simple and efficient way.

Why We Collect Data

We collect minimal necessary data to provide core app features such as property tracking, tenant management, and rent history management.

Learn More

How We Use Information

  • To manage properties and rental units
  • To track tenants and occupancy
  • To maintain rent and payment history inside the app
  • To improve app performance and reliability
  • To fix bugs and enhance user experience

Data We Collect

  • Name, phone number, email (if provided)
  • Property, room, and tenant details added by users
  • App usage and device information
  • Crash reports for stability improvement

Data Sharing

Rentivo does not sell or share personal data with third parties. Data may only be processed through trusted backend services for app functionality.

Data Security

We use secure systems and industry-standard practices to protect user data. However, no digital system can guarantee 100% security.

User Control

  • View and update stored information
  • Request deletion of account and data
  • Stop using the application anytime

Children’s Privacy

Rentivo is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be reflected within the app or Play Store listing.
