Privacy Policy for Rentivo

Effective Date: 25 April 2026

Rentivo is a property management and rental accounting platform designed for managing properties, tenants, rent collection, and financial records in one place.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address
  • Property Data: Building, flat, PG, or room details added by users
  • Tenant Data: Tenant names, rent details, and payment history (if added by owner)
  • Usage Data: App activity, device information, and crash reports

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Manage rental properties and tenants
  • Track rent payments and due amounts
  • Maintain accounting and expense records
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Ensure app stability and security

3. Data Sharing

Rentivo does not sell or rent user data to any third party. We may share limited data only with trusted service providers (such as Firebase) to support app functionality.

4. Payments (Future Feature)

Rentivo is currently free to use. If payment features are introduced in the future, all transactions will be handled securely by third-party payment gateways. We do not store sensitive payment details such as card or UPI credentials.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services. Users may request deletion of their account and data at any time.

7. Third-Party Services

Rentivo may use third-party services such as Firebase for authentication, analytics, and storage. These services follow their own privacy policies.

8. Children’s Privacy

Rentivo is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected in the app or Play Store listing.
